Let visitors to your website give their consent to setting cookies.

This paddlet intends to deal with the EU Directive on Privacy and Electronic Communications that comes into effect in the UK on 26th May 2012. From this date on, you run the risk of enforcement action if you are not compliant or visibly working towards compliance. More information can be found here

Installation and configuration

  • Enable the Cookie paddlet
  • Create an information page on cookies being used on your website.
  • Go to the settings page at of the paddlet to enable and customise the pop-up.
